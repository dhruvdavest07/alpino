SOP 1: Store Opening Procedure
Objective: To ensure the café is fully operational, clean, and ready to serve customers before
opening hours.
Scope
Applies to all Alpino Café outlets and all
opening shift staff.
Responsible Staff
• Store Manager
• Shift Supervisor
• Kitchen Staff
Required Tools & Equipment
• POS terminal
• Cleaning equipment
• Ingredient prep containers
• Temperature log sheets
Step-by-Step Procedure
1. Store Entry and Safety Check
5 min
Manager unlocks store 30 minutes before opening time. Turn on the main power supply.
Inspect the kitchen area for safety issues.
2. Equipment Activation
5 min
Turn on: coffee machine, blender stations, refrigerator and display units, POS system.
3. Ingredient Preparation
10 min
Kitchen staff prepare: chopped vegetables, sandwich fillings, smoothie bases.
4. POS System Check
3 min
Verify: menu items active, pricing updated, online aggregators synced.
5. Store Readiness
5 min
Check: dining area cleanliness, takeaway packaging availability, beverage cups stocked.
Quality Control Checklist
✓ All equipment functioning✓ Ingredients prepped and stored correctly
✓ POS system operational✓ Store cleaned and organised
Total Expected Opening Time: 25–30 minutes

SOP 2: Staff Uniform and Grooming Standards
Objective: To maintain a professional and hygienic appearance for all Alpino Café employees.
Scope
Applies to all front-of-house and kitchen
staff.
Responsible Staff
• Store Manager
• Shift Supervisor
Required Uniform
• Alpino branded t-shirt
• Black trousers
• Apron
• Closed-toe shoes
• Hair net (kitchen staff)
Grooming Standards
Employees must maintain clean hands and nails, avoid jewellery while handling food, keep
hair tied back, and maintain overall hygiene.
Step-by-Step Procedure
1. Pre-Shift Uniform Check
2 min / employee
Supervisor checks uniform cleanliness, personal hygiene, and name badge visibility.
Quality Control Checklist
✓ Uniform clean and ironed✓ Hair tied or covered
✓ Hands sanitised before shift✓ No jewellery in the kitchen area

SOP 4: Order Processing Using POS (PetPooja)
Objective: To ensure accurate order processing and efficient coordination between the cashier
and kitchen.
Scope
Applies to all POS operators and kitchen
staff.
Responsible Staff
• Cashier
• Kitchen Staff
Step-by-Step Procedure
1. Order Entry
30 sec
Cashier inputs order into the POS system.
2. Kitchen Notification
Instant
Order automatically appears on the kitchen display system or order ticket printer.
3. Order Preparation
Kitchen staff begin preparation immediately.
• Beverages — 90 seconds
• Sandwiches — 2 minutes
• Protein bowls — 3 minutes
4. Order Completion
< 3 min total
Kitchen staff mark order as completed. Cashier calls customer number.
Quality Control Checklist
✓ Order matches POS receipt✓ Items prepared correctly
✓ Order served within time target✓ Packaging correct for takeaway

SOP 9: Ingredient Storage and Cold Chain Management
Objective: To maintain ingredient freshness, safety, and quality through proper storage and
temperature control.
Scope
Applies to all kitchen and inventory staff
handling food ingredients.
Responsible Staff
• Store Manager
• Kitchen Staff
• Shift Supervisor
Required Equipment
• Refrigerators
• Freezers
• Temperature log sheet
• Airtight storage containers
Confidential — Internal Use Only                                                                    11
----------------Page (10) Break----------------
ALPINO CAFÉ · Standard Operating Procedures                                 QSR Operations Handbook
Step-by-Step Procedure
1. Ingredient Receiving
3 min / batch
Inspect ingredient quality upon delivery. Check packaging integrity and expiry date.
2. Temperature Check
1 min
Measure the temperature of refrigerated items before storage.
• Refrigerated products: 2°C – 5°C
• Frozen products: −18°C or below
3. Storage Organisation
3 min
Store ingredients by category (dairy, vegetables, protein, sauces). Label all containers
with the date of storage.
4. FIFO Implementation
Continuous
Follow First-In-First-Out (FIFO) principle. Older stock must be used before newer
stock.
Quality Control Checklist
✓ All ingredients labelled with date✓ Refrigerator temperature logged every 4 hours
✓ FIFO followed✓ No expired ingredients in storage

SOP 10: Waste Management and Expiry Control

Scope
Applies to all staff responsible for ingredi-
ent handling.
Responsible Staff
• Store Manager
• Kitchen Staff
Required Equipment
• Waste bins
• Expiry tracking log
• Inventory system
Step-by-Step Procedure

3 min
Kitchen staff check ingredient expiry at the start of each shift.
2. Waste Segregation
2 min
Separate waste into: food waste, packaging waste, and recyclable materials.

1 min

SOP 12: Store Closing Procedure
Objective: To ensure the store is cleaned, inventory is secured, and systems are properly shut
down at the end of the day.
Scope
Applies to all closing shift staff.
Responsible Staff
• Store Manager
• Shift Supervisor
• Kitchen Staff
Step-by-Step Procedure

5 min

5 min

5. POS Closing
2 min
Generate daily sales report from POS.

✓ Refrigerators checked and closed✓ Store locked securely
Total Expected Closing Time: 20–25 minutes
